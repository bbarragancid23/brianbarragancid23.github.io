Last Stand

Description

A fast-paced top-down zombie survival game developed in Pygame during a 24-hour hackathon by a four-person team. Last Stand blends tight arcade-style shooting with atmospheric design and intelligent enemy behavior. Built from the ground up in a single day, the project demonstrates rapid prototyping, collaborative development, and the integration of gameplay systems, original art, and sound design into a single cohesive experience.

Features

  • Atmospheric Fog-of-War Visual System
    • Centers the player within a dynamic fog effect that limits visibility and obscures approaching enemies. This design choice heightens tension and forces reactive gameplay, as zombies can emerge from darkness with little warning, reinforcing the survival-horror atmosphere.
  • Intelligent Zombie Pathfinding
    • Implements shortest-path navigation that allows enemies to consistently track the player while respecting environmental constraints. Zombies dynamically route around walls and obstacles, creating believable pursuit behavior rather than simple linear movement.
  • Fully Original Art & Sound Design
    • Features custom pixel-art characters, animations, and sound effects created entirely in-house. Every visual and auditory element—from player movement to zombie feedback—was designed to support the game’s tone and reinforce moment-to-moment immersion
